Spaghetti & Meatballs

This spaghetti and meatball recipe is easy enough for a busy weeknight — and it is a family favorite, too!

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Fresh Basil
  • Parsely
  • Oregano
  • Meat
  • Parmigiano-Reggiano Cheese
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Preheat the oven to 350°F
  • In a large bowl, whisk together the egg, basil, parsley, oregano, salt, pepper, garlic and water. Add the meat.
  • Roll the mixture into golf ball-sized meatballs and place on an ungreased baking sheet. Bake for about 10 minutes.
  • In the meantime, bring the marinara sauce to a simmer in a large skillet.
  • While the meatballs are cooking, bring a large pot of well-salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook until al dente. Drain, then toss with the sauce and meatballs
